=================== Installation =================== 
Step one:
Go to C:\Users\USERNAME\AppData\Roaming\Wonderdraft\themes

Step two:
Place the .wonderdraft_theme file in this folder.

Step three:
Start Wonderdraft, open/create a map, click on Themes (top bar) and select the theme.

=================== Lava texture =================== 
Step one:
Go to C:\Users\USERNAME\AppData\Roaming\Wonderdraft\assets

Step two:
Place the folder Avoro - Gates of Hell in the assets folder

Step three:
Start Wonderdraft, open/create a map, click on Water texture.
